# Apple TV Remote Changelog

## [Initial Version] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

- Full Apple TV remote (d-pad, playback, volume, text input) over the Companion protocol — zero external installs
- Bonjour discovery + on-screen PIN pairing, with manual-IP fallback
- App launcher grid backed by the device's live app list
- Play/Pause and Home as hotkey-able commands, plus menu bar quick controls
- Sleep/wake power control
- AI tools: press keys, launch apps, and deep-link shows ("play Rick and Morty on Netflix") via Raycast AI
